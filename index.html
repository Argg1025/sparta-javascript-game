Air Hockey

Instructions

Each player must try to score in the opposite players goal (indicated by the semi-circles either side), first to three goals wins.

Player 1 controls: w = up, s = down, a = left and d = right.

Player 2 controls: i = up, k = down, j = left and l = right.
